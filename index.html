Purdue Purity Test

Caution: This is not a bucket list. Completion of all items on this test will likely result in academic probation, suspension, or expulsion.


The Purdue Purity Test serves as a way for students to bond and track their experiences throughout their time at Purdue University. It's a voluntary and anonymous chance for students to reflect on their individual university journey. This is inspired by the original rice purity test, which is found here.
